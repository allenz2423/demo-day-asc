Wasted Electronics

Every year, 6.92 million pounds of e-waste is created. Now imagine how much we can cut down on pollution if people got together and started properly disposing of their electronics. This would drastically reduce the amount of e-waste that enters the environment.

Why Should You Care?

  • Electronics contain toxic substances such as lead and mercury
  • They contain valuable raw materials such as gold and copper
  • Recycling helps save energy and raw materials for the future
